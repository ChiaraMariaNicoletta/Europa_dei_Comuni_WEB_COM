ABOUT US

We connect Lombardy's territories to European institutions and promote active participation in European decision-making processes.

We are the European Department of ANCI Lombardia. We work to facilitate access to funding opportunities and promote awareness on various European issues.

Our mission is to enhance the skills and planning capabilities of local authorities, promote local experiences, and create sustainable networks and partnerships at the European level.

Our work serves local communities so that they can make the most of the challenges and opportunities arising from European Union policies and funding.

We believe that a Europe that is closer to its territories is only possible if local governments have a voice, the tools, and the skills to actively participate in European policies. The Europe Department is the association's point of reference for translating this commitment into concrete results for Lombardy's municipalities.
