NEWS 9 Feb 2026

LinkedIn “Europa dei Comuni”: ANCI Lombardia’s Europe Office

The new LinkedIn “Europa dei Comuni” page is now online, a showcase page by ANCI Lombardia dedicated to the activities of the Europe Office.
The page was created with the aim of strengthening communication and sharing European opportunities aimed at municipalities and local authorities in Lombardy, to stay up to date, share best practices, and strengthen dialogue between the local and European levels.

We believe that a Europe that is closer to its territories is only possible if local governments have a voice, the tools, and the skills to actively participate in European decision-making processes and policies.

CIRCULAR 29 Jan 2026

Appeal in Support of Ukrainian Local and Regional Authorities – Energy Emergency

For your information, please note that the President of the European Committee of the Regions and the Chair of the CoR Working Group on Ukraine have issued a letter containing an urgent appeal for support for Ukrainian cities and regions affected by the serious energy emergency.
Interested entities are invited to consider contributing by supplying energy equipment, in accordance with the procedures set out in the attached communication. We also encourage the maintenance and strengthening of existing direct partnerships with Ukrainian counterparts as an effective means of concrete solidarity.

NEWS 12 Nov 2025

European programming, Bianchi: “Europe cannot do without living in cities”

The Deputy Secretary of ANCI Lombardy and member of the Committee of the Regions Matteo Luigi Bianchi at the Conference on EUI and European programming in Bologna.

On the opening day of the 42nd ANCI Assembly in Bologna, EUIs, urban innovation and regeneration programs were the focus of a meeting that centered on the experiences of municipalities.
Bianchi emphasized how this year the Association arrives at this event with a significant new development: ANCI has become the national Urban Contact Point for the EUI Program. It is a clear political signal: Europe sees Italian municipalities as reliable players, capable of innovating, regenerating, and experimenting.

NEWS 27 Oct 2025

Event: “Cohesion policies, European funds, and territorial development” October 27

ANCI Lombardia proposes an experimental workshop to Minister Foti to support municipalities in the use of EU resources.

The future of cohesion policies, the prospects for direct European funds, and the challenges for sustainable territorial development were the focus of the meeting “Cohesion policies, direct European funds, and territorial development,” held today at the headquarters of ANCI Lombardia, attended by, among others, the President of ANCI Lombardia, Mauro Guerra, the Minister for European Affairs, the PNRR and Cohesion Policies, Tommaso Foti, and the Undersecretary to the Presidency with responsibility for International and European Relations of the Lombardy Region, Raffaele Cattaneo.

NEWS 17 Oct 2025

ANCI Lombardy at the European Week of Regions and Cities 2025

Deputy Secretary Matteo Luigi Bianchi speaks in Brussels on innovation and European standardization

During the “Shaping Standards Together” meeting, organized by the European AFCOS project and dedicated to the theme of standardization as a tool for strengthening innovation, competitiveness, and territorial cohesion, Bianchi stated: "Standardization is not just a technical issue, but a way to build trust and collaboration between territories, businesses, and citizens. When we share common rules, we also build a common vision of Europe—one that is closer to the territories and more capable of transforming innovation into concrete development."
ANCI Lombardia thus confirms its commitment to promoting the active participation of Lombardy and Italian municipalities in European policies, fostering dialogue between local authorities, the productive world, and EU institutions.
